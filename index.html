iReceptionist

Technology: HTML, CSS, javaScript

iReceptionist is a web application that businesses would use when it offers services that require a check-in process. iReceptionist will simplify the process for business owners, business visitors and the receptionist.

Worked with a team of 11 engineers to polish an exsisting web application. I initally was the leader of the documentation team but I joined the front-end team due to the lack of skilled engineers. Using Agile methodology, our team have weekly iterations depending on the needs of our client. I coded the front-end page for the visitor page as well as the landing page. In this project, we used Heroku to deploy the application. Through scrums and stand-up meetings, our final product was well catered to our client.
Lunch People

The Lunch People social web application that bridges all departments of a company together through lunch. We pair you with with coworkers from any department, and give you personal profiles to view.
